## 7. No SoundExchange Registration

**SEVERITY:** ðŸ”´ Critical (if getting radio play) / ðŸŸ¡ Warning (if not)

**TRIGGER:**
- SoundExchange = no AND
- Has released recordings

**CONSEQUENCE:**
- Missing digital radio royalties (Pandora, SiriusXM, etc.)
- SiriusXM pays ~$35 per spin total (split between Rights Owner and Featured Artist)
- Amount varies significantly by genre/exposure
- This is the MOST valuable royalty if getting SXM play

**IMPORTANT CONTEXT:**
SoundExchange has TWO separate registrations:
1. **Rights Owner** - Who owns the master recording
2. **Featured Artist** - Who is the main performer on the recording

Most indie artists need BOTH registrations to collect 100%.

Also critical: You're entitled to these royalties even on COVER SONGS. If you recorded and performed a cover of someone else's song, you own the Rights Owner and Featured Artist royalties for YOUR recording (not the underlying composition royalties - those go to the songwriter).

**PLAN ACTION:**
- "Register with SoundExchange as BOTH Rights Owner AND Featured Artist"
- "Even if you've registered once, verify you're registered on both sides"
- "This applies to cover songs too - you own the master royalties for your recordings"
- Note: "SXM spins are extremely valuable (~$35/spin total at 100% ownership)"

## 16. PRO - Only Registered as Writer (Missing Publisher Share)

**SEVERITY:** ðŸ”´ Critical

**TRIGGER:**
- PRO member = yes AND
- Publishing admin = none AND
- Has NOT created own publishing entity with PRO

**IMPORTANT CONTEXT:**
Performance royalties are split 50/50 between **Writer** and **Publisher**. This is true at ASCAP, BMI, and all PROs.

- If you're signed to a publisher, they collect the publisher 50%
- If you have a publishing admin (Songtrust, CD Baby Pro, etc.), they collect publisher share on your behalf
- If you're a DIY indie artist with NO publisher and NO admin, **you're only collecting 50% of your performance royalties**

The solution: Create your own publishing entity with your PRO. This is FREE and takes ~15 minutes. You're essentially becoming your own publisher to collect both halves.

**CONSEQUENCE:**
- Missing 50% of all performance royalties
- This affects EVERY song, EVERY play
- At $100/month in writer royalties, you're missing another $100/month in publisher royalties
- Many artists don't realize this for years

**DETECTION:**
- Ask follow-up: "Have you set up a publishing entity with your PRO, or are you only registered as a writer?"
- Most will say "What's a publishing entity?" = they're missing it

**PLAN ACTION:**
- Priority 1: "Set up a publishing entity with your PRO immediately"
- "This is FREE and takes about 15 minutes"
- "You could be missing 50% of your performance royalties"
- "At ASCAP: Go to Member Access â†’ Create a Publisher. At BMI: Apply for publisher affiliation."
- "Once created, you need to register your songs under your publisher entity too"
- Estimate: Double their current PRO estimate

## 18. Cover Song Download Licensing

**SEVERITY:** ðŸŸ¢ Info

**TRIGGER:**
- Has released cover songs AND
- Cover songs available for download (iTunes, Amazon, etc.)

**IMPORTANT CONTEXT:**
For streaming services (Spotify, Apple Music), you do NOT need a mechanical license - The MLC's blanket license covers it. However, for DOWNLOADS (paid downloads), you DO still need a mechanical license.

**CONSEQUENCE:**
- If releasing covers for download without license, potentially liable
- Most distributors now handle this automatically (DistroKid charges $12/song)

**PLAN ACTION:**
- "If you have cover songs on download platforms, verify mechanical licenses were obtained"
- "DistroKid and others handle this for a fee - check that it was included"
- "For streaming only, no action needed (blanket license covers it)"
